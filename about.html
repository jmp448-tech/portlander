About Us

Since 2015

Desire Meets A New Taste

Portlander Fermentation Lab was born in the heart of Buenos Aires, where science and craft collide in every bottle. What started as a group of friends experimenting with homebrews quickly turned into a full-blown fermentation lab dedicated to pushing boundaries.

From bold IPAs to wild fermented creations, we honor Argentina’s creative spirit by crafting drinks that are as vibrant and complex as the culture around us. Every brew is local, intentional, and proudly made with soul.
